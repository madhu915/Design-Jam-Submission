The Girl Who Stood Up for Education and was Shot by the Taliban...

  • 1997
    Born in Mingora, Pakistan on July 12, 1997, her father was determined to support his beloved daughter's life with no gender bias.
  • 2008
    Taliban took control over the town and banned several things imposing harsh punishments for citizens who disobeyed. Eventually girls were restricted from attending school, as a result Malala could no longer meet her friends. Well, Malala decide to make her voice heard and so she began blogging about living under the Taliban's threats to deny her education and consistently emphasised the right to education for all women..
  • 2011
    Her activism gained momentum and as a result bagged a nomination for the International Children's Peace Prize in 2011. She was awarded Pakistan's National Youth Peace Prize .
  • 2012
    October 2012, on her way home from school, a masked gunman boarded the bus and demanded to know her identity. He shot her on the left side of my head, as a result she suffered severe repercussions leaving her in a critical condition. She was flown to a hospital in Birmingham, England to receive further intensive treatment. At this crucial point, the entire world prayed for her recovery.
  • 2013
    Yousafzai gave a speech at the United Nations on her 16th birthday highlighting her focus on education and women's rights, urging world leaders to change their policies.
    "The extremists were, and they are, afraid of books and pens. The power of education frightens them. They are afraid of women... Let us pick up our books and pens. They are our most powerful weapons."
  • 2017
    Enduring all harships, she strived to reach her goals and fought to ensure all girls receive 12 years of free quality education.. She began studying at the Oxford University..
  • 2020
    June 2020, amidst the ongoing pandemic she graduated with a degree in Philosophy, Politics and Economics successfully!!! She intends to constantly work towards her goals and persistently fight for education and equality no matter what!!
